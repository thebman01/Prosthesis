Other Ideas to Make an Even Bigger Change in this Industry


What do our brains do to make our hands, legs, fingers, ec. move?All the things we do while moving are put in a category which we call our motor skills. This works because our brain sends us "messages" from the pyramidal motor pathway which originates from the cerebral cortex or the brainstem. The peripheral nervous system is (as it sounds) the nerves along your ganglia (which is on the outside of the brain) and your spinal cord. Some nerves on the spinal cord are connected to your arm's nerves/muscles. The nerves in your arms are connected to the cervical nerves which send waves of impulses to and from the muscles in the hands, the forearms, and the skin of the hands.
